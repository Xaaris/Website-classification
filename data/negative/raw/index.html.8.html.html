DE Job Market

The DE Job Market (www.de-jobmarket.com) platform is an efficient source for domestic and international talents, and the preeminent platform for reaching professionals with a German background. On DE Job Market companies will gain access to our big pool of highly qualified candidates and elevate the company profile to jobseekers.

Companies will also benefit from:

  • High Profile - Qualified company referrals and a big pool of candidates with a German background
  • Effortlessness - Online and Mobile job posting possibilities and candidate search

  • Employer Branding - Job listings with complete company logo, profile and link to homepage
  • Additional Exposure - Banner advertisement and special advertising programs

  • Our Expertise and a well-established Brand - Job market services since 2002
  • Fast Reaction Time - Job listings and advertisements within 2 work days

Many companies have successfully utilized the DE Job Market in order to efficiently fill vacant temporary (internship) or permanent occupational positions, leaving us with impressive website statistics:

Monthly visits: 77,000
Monthly page views: 500,000
Average views per job offer: 700
