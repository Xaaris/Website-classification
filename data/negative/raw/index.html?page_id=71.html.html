Informations for applicants

Bewerbungsformular herunterladen

Dear Applicant,

Many thanks for your interest in applying for a grant from Künstlerhof Schreyahn.
You may apply for a composer or literature grant lasting three, six or nine months. Four studios are available for occupation troughout the year. The grant is monthly 1.400 €.

Please note the following conditions:

1. The closing date for composer applications is January 2019. The closing date for literature applications is January 2019. Please send your documents to

Samtgemeinde Lüchow (Wendland)
Abteilung 4
Bewerbung Künstlerhof
Postfach 1342
29433 Lüchow (Wendland)

2. All your documents should be sent in a single package. Incomplete applications will be returned without assessment by the jury. For the literature application please send the material in triplicate.

3. A charge of 25,– € is made for handling the applications. This sum should be paid into one of the accounts listed below. Please do not send a cheque.

4. On customs declarations, please write: “Not merchandise”. Please consider by your application, that the Künstlerhof is situated in a secluded village with only 100 inhabitants. The road and rail networks are slightly developed. Therefore you need a car or a bike for going shopping in the surrounding towns. In the past these facts have gotten on some artists nerves.

You will be informed about the jury´s decision and the possible date the grant period starts in writing – if possible in coordination with you. Please refrain from making any enquiries.

Yours sincerely
Hubert Schwedland
Samtgemeindebürgermeister
