(Senior) Stereo Vision Engineer

Role

You will play a key role in our computer vision team. You will lead our stereo vision framework development and you will be responsible for providing a high-quality stereo vision system. Your ideas and development in this area will be crucial for the quality and success of our product.

Responsibilities

  • Lead and develop our stereo-vision framework with focus on novel and accurate computer vision algorithms
  • Develop algorithms for stereo and 3D imaging, feature extraction, sensor fusion and alignment (such as Lidar with imagery), complex object segmentation, camera calibration and 3D reconstruction
  • Increase accuracy of stereo vision cameras
  • Apply machine learning to computer vision problems
  • Prototype hardware and software solutions for detection, classification, and recognition
  • Stay up-to-date with latest development in computer vision and implement new approaches

Requirements

  • University degree in Computer Science, Computer Vision or Robotics with a strong focus on Software Engineering.
  • Proficient programming skills to develop efficient algorithms in C/C++
  • Understanding of 2D and 3D visualization libraries and GPU implementations
  • Experience with machine learning, Bayesian filtering, information theory and/or 3D geometry
  • Experience in image processing especially in the field of complex object segmentation
  • Experience training deep convolutional and/or recurrent neural networks is a plus
  • Experience with 3D sensor data (ToF, Stereo, Structured Light, LiDAR) is a plus
  • Strong theoretical and practical background in Computer Vision, Image analysis, specifically in Stereo Vision, Probabilistic Graphical Models, Optical
  • Start-up thinking and never-give-up mentality

We enjoy creative autonomy while having full financial support from our parent company, a global technology leader. We are working in small, agile and nimble teams that are focused on excellence. We love to experiment with the latest technology and crazy ideas.
