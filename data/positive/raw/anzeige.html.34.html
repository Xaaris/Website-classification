Plant Leader (f/m)

Location: Pleinfeld near Nuremberg
Job number: 170771

As a good strategic thinker you effectively create, communicate and execute long range strategic and tactical plans, drive operational excellence and continuous improvement activities. Your ability to influence and hold others accountable in a complex global environment helps you to lead, motivate, mentor and integrate the activities of all local operations related functions to fulfill business objectives. This includes prioritizing needs, leveraging skills and ensuring appropriate structure and resources for all aspects of the plant operations. Last but not least:

  • Promoting safe working conditions, while ensuring the building is maintained to a high standard of quality and cleanliness.
  • Accountable for compliant operation of the plant and for the safety/protection of associates, contractors and visitors.
  • Ensuring that the appropriate processes and equipment (e.g. training, personal protective equipment, environmental controls, education, mechanical systems etc.) are in place and operating properly to meet legal requirements and achieve the health, safety and environmental goals and standards of the plant.

Your profile:

  • Minimum of 10 years’ broad operations experience in a complex global environment.
  • Minimum of 5 years in a senior leadership role with proven capability for leading and influencing others to meet business needs, optimize manufacturing costs, and enable employee development, ideally in an AS9100 or TS16949 certified environment.
  • A degree in engineering, operations, supply chain management or similar qualification.
  • Proven track record for translating manufacturing performance metrics into continuous improvements within safety, cost, quality, on time delivery, and other key business objectives.
  • Track record for leading and executing on regional and global projects.
  • Working knowledge of key technical and functional capabilities within manufacturing, including environment, health, and safety regulations, ideally ISO 14001 experience.
  • Fluent English and German.
