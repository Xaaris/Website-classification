(Senior) Sensor Fusion Engineer

Role

You will be part of the core system development. You will develop stable and high quality software for our core system Application, which has to work 24/7. You will be responsible to develop an intelligent sensor fusion & self-recovering system, which uses artificial intelligence and autonomous agents.

  • Establish and develop an automated reporting system (e.g. system health, user interaction)
  • Develop an advanced and intelligent fraud prevention system
  • Stay up to date on technology trends & technologies and bring them into our development

  • University degree in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering or Robotics
  • Advanced knowledge in C++
  • Experience in different machine learning algorithms
  • Experience in development of autonomous agents is a big plus
  • Experience training deep convolutional and/or recurrent neural networks is a plus
  • Strong software architecture skills
  • Start-up thinking and never-give-up mentality
