We are building a new product in a new category. We strongly believe in deep learning and smart sensors. We want to disrupt the traditional checkout of the food retail industry and become market leader in A.I. based checkout systems. Our first A.I. based checkout device is called Trayzi. (Senior) DevOps Engineer

Role

Work collaboratively with computer vision engineers, system engineers and server experts to ensure a high available cloud infrastructure for our development and production system. Furthermore, you will be responsible for identifying synergies between our cross-functional team and make sure to use them.

Responsibilities

  • You are responsible for operating our infrastructure and the development of any necessary tool helping on this task.
  • Take over responsibility for Continuous Integration tool chain.
  • Identify areas where we can use open source software, services and other solutions to reduce the amount of code and optimize our development.
  • Identify optimization potentials both in processes and tools and strive to implement them.
  • Your goal will be to automate every manual process.
  • Stay up to date on technology trends and bring them into our development.
  • Attend technology trade shows and conferences to stay-up-to date.
  • Establish full automated update mechanism and ensures faster releases

Requirements

  • University degree in Computer Science.
  • Strong Java coding experience
  • Python is a plus
  • Experience with Continuous Integration tools (Jenkins, TeamCity)
  • Strong Cloud-Computing (AWS, Google Cloud) knowledge
  • Ability to use a wide variety of open source technologies and tools
  • Comfort with frequent, incremental code testing and deployment
  • Keep track of current trends and technologies
  • Networking knowledge (VPN, firewall’s, routing table)
  • Strong software architecture skills
  • Know-how in Spring, hibernate frameworks is a plus
